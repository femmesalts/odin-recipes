BBQ Cabbage

Cabbage is my favorite vegetable - it's so versatile, healthy, and delicious! This is a German braised cabbage recipe with an American twist, which adds barbecue sauce to make a sweet and savory cookout side dish.

Makes 4-6 servings

  1. Cook in a large pan over medium heat until soft:

    • 1 red onion, sliced
    • 2 granny smith apples, peeled, cored, & sliced

  2. Add to pan and cook until cabbage starts to wilt, about 10 minutes:

    • 1 small red cabbage, cored & shredded (about 2 1/2 lbs)
    • salt & pepper to taste

  3. Add to pan and simmer uncovered on medium low until cabbage is tender, about 30 minutes:

    • 1 1/2 cups vegetable broth
    • 1/3 cup apple cider vinegar

  4. Add to pan and simmer until syrupy, about 5 minutes:

    • 1/3 cup barbecue sauce

  5. Remove from heat and stir in butter to serve:

    • 1 tbsp vegan butter

  6. Enjoy!
