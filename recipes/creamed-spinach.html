Creamed Spinach

This vegan creamed spinach was made possible by raw cashews.

Try the green stuff, it's delicious! Don't believe me? Ask the dishes!

Makes 3-4 servings

  1. Soak cashews at least 2 hours. drain and blend with enough water to make it smooth and creamy, about 1/2 cup. Set aside:

    • 1/2 cup raw cashews, pieces or whole

  2. Set aside a bowl of ice water. Bring water to boil in a large pot. Once boiling, add spinach and steam for 30 seconds. Strain and add spinach to ice water to blanch. Stir a few times until cooled and drain. Set aside:

    • 20 oz frozen spinach

  3. In a separate pan, cook cook onion and garlic in butter 5-10 minutes over medium-low heat, or until soft:

    • 2 tbsp vegan butter
    • 1/2 a yellow or white onion, diced
    • 2 cloves garlic

  4. Add the rest to the pan, including the cashew cream and spinach, and cook a few minutes until thick and glossy:

    • 1/2 cup vegan cheese
    • 1/4-1/2 cup non-dairy milk
    • 1 tsp vinegar or lemon juice
    • 1/2 tsp sea salt
    • 1/4 tsp black pepper
    • 1/4 tsp cayenne or nutmeg, whichever you'd prefer

  5. Serve immediately with some crusty bread. Enjoy!
